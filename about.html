OUR STORY

Born From a Passion for Clean Cars

AutoShine started when a group of friends realized that quality car detailing should not require you to drive across the city, wait for hours, and then drive back. We believed there was a better way.

We started with one car, one kit, and one mission — to bring professional-grade detailing directly to our customers. Today we have served hundreds of satisfied car owners across the city, all from the comfort of their own homes.

Every detail we do is treated like it is our own car. We use only premium products, proven techniques, and we never cut corners. Your car deserves the best — and we deliver exactly that.
